# Oxygen-Engine (Archived)

**Status:** Concept archived. Prior research on oxy-fuel gas turbines exists internationally (GE, NET Power, etc.). This repository is kept as a reference for an independent design and as a potential integration with the ShieldAir-Urban and ShieldAir-Mars ecosystems.

## Context

Pure oxygen combustion in gas turbines is not a new idea. From GE patents in 2009 to NET Power's Allam cycle, the scientific and industrial community has been developing this technology for years. The purpose of this repository is not to claim invention, but to document a specific approach:

- **Turbine powered by in-situ oxygen** produced by ShieldAir-Urban towers (urban smog capture) or ShieldAir-Mars (CO₂ extraction from the Martian atmosphere).
- **Mobility application** (ships, trains, trucks) in oxygen-poor environments (high altitude, submarines, Mars), not just stationary power plants.
